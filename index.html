Hello!

My name is Jay Mendapara and I am a programmer, photographer, and a graphic designer. I am currently pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science with a minor in Psychology at Rutgers University. My journey into the world of photography kicked off a few years ago, when I first started sharing my perspective of the world with others. Over time, my objective of photography shifted from sharing my personal experiences to sharing the beauty of the world through nature photography and photojournalism. I am fascinated by landscapes, wildlife, and unique lifestyles so I work hard to integrate those elements into my portfolio. I started programming towards the end of high school, when I discovered the creative potential of a few lines of code. Soon after, I started to develop a strong passion for web development as it embodies creativity and human interaction. Today, I am ready to use what I have learned in school and on my own to overlook real-world projects with a fresh set of eyes. Here is my resume.
